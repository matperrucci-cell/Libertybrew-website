Our story

From Ohio to Montgomery County

Liberty Brew was founded in the heart of Ohio in 2018, based on a simple but ambitious idea: to create an American beer that stands out not only for its taste, but also for its personality. From day one, our goal has been clear: to produce a beer of above-average quality, made from selected ingredients, careful processes and a genuine passion for the art of brewing.

In a short time, thanks to the support of our local community and growing demand from our customers, Liberty Brew has become more than a brand: it has become a symbol of independence, dedication and love for craft beer.

Today, driven by the enthusiasm of those who have chosen us and the belief that quality deserves to be shared, we are looking beyond the borders of Ohio. We are ready to embark on a new adventure: expansion into a new market, Montgomery County, MD.

For us, this step represents much more than just geographical growth. It means bringing our philosophy, our commitment and our taste to a new community, creating connections, experiences and moments to remember.

Liberty Brew is a journey that has just begun, and we want to share it with you, one sip at a time.

Brand experience

Craft Your Brew Experience (CBE)

Discover flavours, build your flavour card

Mini-workshops in DLC stores and premium supermarkets: discover flavours and ingredients, create your own flavour card and taste 17th Star with a brand ambassador. Signage and QR codes provide information on the product's history and calendar, while interactive tastings help customers understand how different hops, malts and aromas come together in the final beer. Each session ends with pairing suggestions and tips to fully enjoy Liberty Brew at home or with friends.
